X-ray¶

X-ray image (study in DICOM terms)

date
Date of the x-ray
accession_no

Field is (mis-)used to store body part.

Values:
  • a: Becken ap (von vorne)
  • b: Becken und LWS von vorne
  • c: Becken Barsony andere Perspektive
  • e: LWS ap
  • f: LWS lateral
  • ef: LWS ap und lateral auf einem Röntgenbild
  • h: HWS lateral
scoring_dates
Sequence of dates when scores have been created for this x-ray
scoring_types

Sequence of types of scores for this image.

Values:
  • rau: RAU score
  • mnyc: mNYC score
